Our Story

Established in 2022, CY Patisserie is a dream realized through a shared passion for exquisite pastries. Our journey is a tale of dedication from skilled pastry chefs who infuse creativity into every treat. Inspired by tradition yet embracing innovation, we craft a unique blend of classic and contemporary delights. As we grow, each customer becomes a cherished part of the sweet narrative that defines CY Patisserie.
